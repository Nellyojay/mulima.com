About Us

ABOUT MULIMA

Mulima is a unique platform that combines a sustainable food ecosystem with financial and AI (Artificial Intelligance) inclusion, aiming to reduce waste of agricultural produce and improve access to affordable food for consumers both in the present and in the future using our savings model.
Mulima stands out by integrating e-commerce, fintech, AI, and agriculture into one system, using a points system that fosters investment and savings within the food sector.
